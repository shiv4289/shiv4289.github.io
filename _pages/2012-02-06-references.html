Here is a list of few books i usually refer while i code :

1) Introduction to Algorithms - by CLRS . Notes and lectures are also available at the MIT website .

2) Art of Programming Contest (for UVA) by Ahmed Shamsul Arefin : The appendix B of this book contains some really useful readymade  codes . Also this is an excellent book to have if you are a uva programmer . Also this contains topic-wise selection of some really good problems to practice at the uva site .

3) Programming Challenges by Steven Skiena . I have watched the video lectures taken by skiena himself and he is really good at it . The lectures are available on web .

4) The C Programming Language by Kernighan and Ritchie . The solution of the exercises for this book is also available . I have got a copy of "The C answer Book " by Clovis L.Tondo and Scott E Gimpel .

5) Data structure by Tennenbaum (and two more authors i guess..) .
